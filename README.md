## Fisher's Cube
* Ua center-orientation-wise: U2 R U R' U R' U' R2 U' R' U R' U R
* Ub center-orientation-wise: R' U' R U' R U R2 U R U' R U' R' U2
* Parity on Right 'corner': R U R' U' R U R' U y' R' U' R U R' U' R
